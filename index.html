Frequently Asked Questions

What is AgenticAITrading.io?

It is an educational project that publishes end-of-day (EOD) technical target alerts generated by an AI-driven analysis model. Each alert lists an entry, stop-loss, and two targets (T1, T2), and we track every call transparently — wins and losses.

Is this investment advice?

No. Nothing on this site is investment, financial, legal, or tax advice. It is published for educational purposes only. You are solely responsible for your own trading decisions. Always do your own research and consider consulting a licensed financial professional.

How is the success rate calculated?

The success rate is the percentage of closed trades that finished as wins, calculated as wins ÷ (wins + losses). Trades that are still open are not included until they close. The success rate is not a guarantee and past performance is not a reflection of future performance.

What do "T1" and "T2" mean?

T1 (Target 1) and T2 (Target 2) are the two profit objectives for a trade. A green check ✅ appears next to a target once price has reached it. "Stop" is the stop-loss level where the setup is considered invalidated.

What does SHORT vs LONG mean?

LONG expects the price to rise (targets are above entry). SHORT expects the price to fall (targets are below entry). The badge next to each ticker shows the direction.

How often is the site updated?

The tables are updated at the end of each trading day. New setups are marked with a gold "NEW" badge and show the date they were opened.

Do you place trades or manage money?

No. We do not place trades, manage money, or accept funds. The site only publishes and monitors educational technical analysis.
